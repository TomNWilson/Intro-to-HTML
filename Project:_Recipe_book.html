Marinara Sauce

  • Time: 15 min
  • Makes: 6 servings
Ingredients Quantity
Olive Oil 1 Tablespoon
Chopped Yellow Onion 1 Cup
Mince Garlic 1 1/2 Teaspoon
Red Wine 1/2 Cup
Crushed Tomamtoes 1 Can
Chopped Parsley 1 Tablespoon
Kosher Salt 1 1/2 Teaspoon
Black Pepper 1/2 Teaspoon

Step 1: Heat the olive oil in a large (12-inch) skillet
Step 2: Add the onion and saute over medium heat until translucent, 5 to 10 minutes.
Step 3: Add the garlic and cook for 1 more minute.
Step 4: Add the wine and cook on high heat, scraping up all the brown bits in the pan, until almost all the liquid evaporates, about 3 minutes.
Step 5: Stir in the tomatoes, parsley, salt, and pepper.
Step 6: Cover, and simmer on the lowest heat for 15 minutes.

Source: Food Network

Pineapple Salse

  • Time: 55 min
  • Makes: 12 servings
Ingredients Quantity
Diced Pineapple 1 Pound
Diced Tomatoes 10
Chopped Cilantro 1 Cup
Chopped Red Onion 1 Cup
Chopped Jalapeno Peppers 3-4
Lime Juice 2 Tablespoons
Salt & Pepper 1 Pinch
Hot Pepper Sauce To Taste

Step 1: In a large bowl, combine pineapple, tomatoes, cilantro, red onion, and jalapenos.
Step 2: Add lime juice to bowl and toss to coat.
Step 3: Season to taste with salt, pepper, and hot pepper sauce.
Step 4: Allow flavors to blend at room temperature for 30 minutes, or chill until serving time.

Source: Culinary Hill
